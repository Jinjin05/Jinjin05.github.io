FAMOUS CHINESE DISHES

1.PEKING ROASTED DUCK

Major Ingredients Minor Ingredients
Duck Cucumbers, Green Onions

"The first dish to taste China"
This dishes come from Beijing, China.


How to Cook PEKING ROASTED DUCK

There are two ways to cook the duck. They are closed-oven style and open-oven style. Duck meat taste juicy and tender in closed-oven style, because the meat is combined well with the fat under the skin. But duck skin will teast crispy in open-oven style.


2.KUNG PAO CHICKEN

Major Ingredients Minor Ingredients
Diced Chicken Dried Chili, Cucumber, Fired Peanuts

A famous Sichuan-style speacialty


How to Cook KUNG PAO CHICKEN


There are three versions of Kung Pao Chicken, each place cooked it differentsly. Guizhou, Shandong, and Sichuan has their own type of Kung Pao Chicken. For most of the time, Kung Pao Chicken is savory and sweet with a mild spicy kick.
